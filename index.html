The Rose

You are the rose that blooms through every storm,
Your stem strong and your heart forever warm.
Within everyt thorn a great story of bravery to behold,
And in every petal a lovely bloom to unfold,
The garden of my life has been graced by you —
Alicia,my Mom in every morning's dew.

💜

Lavender

Like lavender in the evening breeze,
You bring me calm, you bring me ease.
Your lullabies still drift to me at night,
A purple haze of warmth and gentle light.
You scent my life with grace I cannot name —
No gift, no word, could ever say the same.

🌻

Sunflower

You always look towardds the sun on the cloudiest days,
you fill our hearts with gold and shining rays.
Like a sunflower you have a way of always finding the light,
You have made our darkest hours ever bright.
You bring sun to our world and that is true
You bring warmth to our hearts and I love you.

🌼

Orchid

Orchids are rare and that is true,
but so is a mom that is a special as you.
I know you worry and try to keep us safe,
and you show us your love in countless ways.
I love you very much, more than words can say.
My appreciation grows every single day.

🌷

The Tulip

A tulip cups its colours to the sky,
The way you'd hold us close evern when we didn't cry
Bravest person whether facing foe or friend,
When times got tough you taught us to never bend.
And even though we are far apart,
A mother's love, can never leave their child's heart.

🌸

Cosmos

Like cosmos dancing in an open field,
You have given us courage we will always yield.
Your smile your laugh they are as beautiful as flowers,
Your presense your wisdom, they fill my most uncertain hours.
The world is wide, the stars are bright,
But you, dear Mom, are my most treasured light.
